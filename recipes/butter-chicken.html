Butter Chicken

Home

Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish made with tender pieces of chicken cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. The dish is known for its buttery flavor and is often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless, skinless)
  • Tomatoes (pureed)
  • Onions (finely chopped)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Spices
    • Cumin
    • Coriander
    • Garam Masala
    • Kashmiri Red Chili Powder
    • Tumeric Powder
  • Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© onions in butter until golden brown, then add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and simmer until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  5. Add kasuri methi and garnish with cilantro before serving.
